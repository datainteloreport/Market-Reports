Market Drivers Fueling Growth
•	Growing Data Centers and Cloud Computing: With the rapid expansion of data centers and the growing reliance on cloud computing, the need for high-speed and reliable connectivity solutions is surging, boosting the demand for Active Copper Cables.
•	Increased Adoption of High-Speed Internet: The rising penetration of high-speed internet and 5G deployment is driving the need for efficient data transmission, further propelling the market.
•	Cost-Effective Alternative to Optical Fiber: Active Copper Cables offer a cost-effective and power-efficient alternative to optical fiber cables, making them a preferred choice for short-distance, high-speed applications.
Market Restraints
•	Limited Transmission Distance: One of the primary challenges in the ACC market is its limited transmission distance compared to fiber optic cables, restricting its adoption in long-range applications.
•	Heat Dissipation Issues: Higher power consumption can lead to heat generation, affecting cable performance in certain applications.
