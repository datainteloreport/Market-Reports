Market Drivers
•	Rising Environmental Awareness: Consumers and businesses are shifting towards biodegradable and recyclable alternatives, reducing dependence on single-use plastics.
•	Government Initiatives & Policies: Bans on plastic products and incentives for eco-friendly manufacturing are encouraging paper cup production.
•	Growth in the Food & Beverage Industry: The increasing popularity of quick-service restaurants (QSRs), coffee chains, and take-away culture is boosting demand.
•	Changing Consumer Preferences: Urbanization and busy lifestyles are propelling the use of convenient and disposable packaging solutions.
Market Restraints
•	Fluctuations in Raw Material Prices: The volatility in paper pulp costs affects manufacturing expenses and pricing strategies.
•	Recycling Challenges: Although paper cups are more sustainable, issues related to their recyclability due to polyethylene coatings hinder full-scale adoption.
•	Competition from Reusable Alternatives: The rising trend of reusable and compostable cups presents a challenge for market penetration.
