Market Drivers
•	Superior Durability & Oxygen Permeability: RGP lenses last longer than soft lenses and allow more oxygen to pass through, reducing the risk of eye infections and discomfort.
•	Growing Prevalence of Myopia & Astigmatism: Rising vision disorders due to excessive screen time and genetic predisposition are fueling the adoption of RGP lenses.
•	Technological Advancements: Innovations such as hybrid lenses, multifocal RGP lenses, and scleral lenses are improving patient comfort and vision correction efficacy.
Market Restraints
•	Initial Discomfort & Adaptation Period: Many users find it challenging to adapt to RGP lenses due to initial discomfort, which may limit market penetration.
•	Higher Cost Compared to Soft Lenses: The cost factor may deter price-sensitive consumers from switching to RGP lenses, affecting market adoption.
•	Availability of Alternative Vision Correction Methods: The popularity of LASIK and advanced soft contact lenses could restrain market growth.
